About SCS

The The Society of Computer Science of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura (SCS of USJP) is a student-based society that was established in the year 2000 to support computer science students. Its main objectives include promoting computer science as a subject, improving the knowledge of information technology among students as well as encouraging the students to participate in IT events and competitions while facilitating them to gain knowledge and experience from the industry.
